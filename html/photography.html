Photography

Photography is my #1 hobby. I am blown away by the beauty in this world and find great enjoyment in being able to capture it whenever I can. My favorite part of the day to shoot is in the early morning, before most are awake. I shoot with a Sony a6000 and a standard variety of lenses. All photos I take are available for purchase from Etsy and Zenfolio. I built a hub for all information regarding my photography here.

Time Lapse

Time lapse is a form of photography that has been close to my heart since I first picked up a camera. I found it to be the perfect median between photography and videography, satisfying both hobbies at once. All time lapse videos are taken shot by shot with my Sony A6000, edited with Adobe Lightroom and assembled into video using Adobe Premiere.
