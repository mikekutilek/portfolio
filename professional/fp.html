Fantasy Points Tool

Gaining popularity every day, fantasy sports have become a staple of the sports gambling industry. Sports betting is spreading across casinos throughout the country and DFS apps like DraftKings and FanDuel are incredibly popular. Everyone is looking for an edge in these games to earn themselves an extra buck. Fortunately, we can utilize the data at our fingertips to gain this edge. This tool is designed to give fantasy players a better way to manage their teams. The tool looks at the most important underlying statistics for each league and compares it to the fantasy points for that player. The idea is to project future performance and identify buy low and sell high candidates.

All data provided by Baseball Savant and Fangraphs
